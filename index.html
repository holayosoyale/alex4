DEVELOPMENT OF A HYBRID CUSTOMER SERVICE APPLICATION FOR THE COMPANY ATLACONET

Background

Based on the registration of customers within the company over the last two years, the irregularities within a system of registration of reports and installations within the telecommunications company atlacoNET, has created a drop in both the installations programs and an increase in the number of service cancellations.

Problem statement

For every company customers are a fundamental part and the reason for their existence, and for atlacoNET company is no exception, hence the importance of providing attention to the problems presented in the service.

Objectives

Administra The objective is to develop a hybrid application, that is to say, that is multiplatform, that allows to improve the times of attention to the client so much for report of failure and installations of the service for the company atlacoNET

Research questions

p- What is a hybrid application and what is it for? - How are hybrid applications created and what are the tools that allow their development? - How much time is it intended to have at least with the application ready? - How much percentage decrease in cancellations will be obtained with the use of the application?
